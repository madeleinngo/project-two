About This Website

Hi, I'm Madelein. I'm a college student who created this website for class and also for personal reference in my Gordon Ramsay Restaurant recipe Master Class.

I first became interested in the class through this iconic Youtube Video ad.

After that, I put it in my mind to one day sign up for the MasterClass and learn from Gordon Ramsay himself. You see, I've always been a fan of Gordon. It's thanks to his videos that I was able to pass my finals in high school!

I heard from a friend that there was a special student discount MasterClass subscription for only $1 that day. Naturally, I took it.

However, after a few times browing the website and watching videos, I found the Master Class website to be too cluttered and overly sophisticated for my use, so I decided to find a more me way of learning from Gordon.

This website features fun, bright colors and modern font to be friendly to broke college students like me or anyone who appreciates the break from the Master Class sophistication.

I hope you enjoy your stay and if you have any questions, just send me an email!
