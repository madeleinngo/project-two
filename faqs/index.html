FAQ

How do you get this content?

I got a year subscription to Masterclass through a special one-day deal for students, only for $1. The recipies themselves are taken from the recipe notes given in the class. You can also access the original materials if you have a Masterclass subscription or free trial.

What skill level are these recipes for?

The Masterclass itself is intended for home cooks that have the basics down from the first Gordon Ramsay Master Class and want to move onto practical recipes.

But, my personal cooking skill level isn't too high, so with some solid practice anyone can use these recipes and achieve good results.
